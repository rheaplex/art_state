Art.

The state of Art is hereby declared. For now its government must sit in exile.

Head of State.

The Head Of State of Art is called The Curator. They appoint themself and must re-appoint themself every two years.

Parliament.

The parliament consists of four Critics, one for each corner of the state. They are to appoint themselves every four years. If no appointment can be reached then parliament shall be dissolved and elections called, except in the case of death or initial appointment where The Curator must appoint the required new Critic(s).

Holidays, Festivals.

The festival of Opening Night shall be held regularly, with merry-making, vitriol and free alcohol by order.
There are no holidays. Art never rests.

Citizenship.

Citizens of the state of Art are known as "Artists". Citizens of Art gain no priveledges or responsibilities by virtue of their citizenship.
Art welcomes applications for joint citizenship.

Laws.

The laws of Art are the laws of aesthetics and composition. Whilst all volumes are not created equal, we must strive to make them harmonious in their parts.

Defense/Non-Aggression Treaties.

Art is eager to enter into diplomatic, military and trade treaty negotiations with other small states.

Recognition.

Art is eager to enter into mutual recognition treaties with other small states.

Territory.

Art will purchase a 1 x 2 mm square of land from another small state to be the nation's territory.

Map.

A survey of Art's territory will be produced at 1:10 scale, making Art the only country to be smaller than its maps.

Flag.

The flag of Art will be its map, and vice versa.

National Anthem.

Cage's 4.32 is the national anthem of Art.

GNP, National Debt and Resources.

The worth of Art cannot be measured monetarily.

Internet Top-Level Domain.

Art will propose .art as its Internet top-level domain name. If this is unavailable, .rt or .at will be accepted.
